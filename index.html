Dragonball Z Stats

This graph shows that from the data gathered Men outnumber Women almost 3 times as much. And it shows that males are more likely to watch the action animated show than women.

This graph is showing the average age of each gender who watches thier favourite series of Dragonball z. When the graph selector is changed to each different series you can see average age goes up. The older series of Dragon ball which short for BALL is quite popular with the eldery group of participants. Where as the newer series of Dragon ball Super, short for SUPER, it attracts a lot of the younger participants as well as some of the older ones.

This Piechart shows a view of how many Males compared to Females watch the animated show. Out of 201 participants 157 where Males and 43 where Female.

This graph below shows the correlation between how old the people who participated in this data and how many years they have been watching the Dragonball z francise. It also shows that the older they are the longer they have been a fan and been watching the show.
